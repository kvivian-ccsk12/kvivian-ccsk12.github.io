# Definition
A [quadrilateral](Quadrilateral.md) with two pairs of opposite, [parallel](Parallel_Lines.md) sides.
# Properties
1. Opposing sides are [congruent](Congruence.md).
2. Opposing [angles](Angle.md) are congruent.
3. Consecutive angles are [supplementary](Supplementary_Angles.md).
4. [Diagonals](Diagonal.md) of a parallelogram [bisect](Bisection.md) each other.
