# Definition
The inclination to one another of two [lines](Line.md) in a plane which meet each other.
When the lines containing the angles are straight, the angle is called *rectilinear*.
# Types
## *Straight* angle
One formed on a straight line.
## *Right* angle
Half of a straight angle
## *Obtuse* angle 
One which lies between a straight angle and a right angle
## *Acute* angle 
One which is less than a right angle
