# Definition
A [quadrilateral](Quadrilateral.md) with one pair of opposite, [parallel](Parallel_Lines.md) sides.
# Properties
1. The [midsegment](Midsegment.md) of the trapezoid is equal to half the sum of the length of the bases
	1. This can be viewed as finding the [average](Arithmetic_Mean.md) length of the two bases.
	2. For trapezoid $ABCD$ this would give us the equation $$\overline{EF} = \frac{1}{2}\left(\overline{AB} + \overline{CD}\right)$$
