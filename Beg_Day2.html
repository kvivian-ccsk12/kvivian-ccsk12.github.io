Pawn Promotion

Reaching the Endzone

Notes:

  • A pawn can become any piece (aside from a King) when it reaches the end.
  • Most of the time you're going to want to make a Queen.
  • The piece doesn't have to already have been captured. Make 9 Queens if you can!

Castling

Building a Fort for Your King

Notes:

  • You cannot castle while in check, through check, or into check.
  • You can only castle if the King and rook you're castling with haven't moved.
  • The King only ever moves two squares.
  • Tournament Rule: Touch your King first when castling.
