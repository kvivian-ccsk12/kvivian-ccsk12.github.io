# Definition
A [quadrilateral](Quadrilateral.md) with two pairs of [congruent](Congruence.md), consecutive sides.
# Properties
1. [Diagonals](Diagonal.md) are [perpendicular](Perpendicular_Lines.md).
2. One pair of congruent, opposite [angles](Angle.md).
	1. In particular this will be the pair of angles between the non-congruent sides
